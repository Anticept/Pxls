FAQ

How do I appeal a ban?
First make sure you've read the rules in the info panel, then contact us on Discord or send an appeal to our google forms.
How do I report bugs?
Submit them to the #game-sugestions channel in the discord. If it's an exploit, feel free to PM one of the developers directly.
How to get 2/6 pixels?
Wait longer, pixels gain passively over time.
Why does it take so long to get more pixels?
This is by design, it's much more efficient to place them as soon as you get them. Pixel stacking is meant to give you a bit of a bump if you go AFK for a while. It takes around 40 minutes to get a full stack of 6/6 pixels, however it only takes around 3 minutes to place 6 pixels on their own.
Why does it say 0/6 placeable?
Pixels gain passively over time, once you place a pixel you're on cooldown until you can place again.
Why is the cooldown different now than it was yesterday?
The cooldown is dynamic and changes based on how many people are online. More people means longer cooldown.
How do I make a template?
Most people use the 3rd party tool PxlsFiddle to turn pixel art into a template. If you need to make pixel art, you can try using Piskel to create sprites then use those sprites in PxlsFiddle.
How do I move a template?
Hold CTRL (or OPTION on mac) while click-and-dragging to reposition as needed. You can also tap-and-hold the top left corner where you want to move the template to and click "Move Template Here" in the resulting popup.
What is the virginmap?
The virginmap shows what pixels are still virgin.
What is a virgin pixel?
A virgin pixel is a background pixel that has not been drawn on by a user yet.
Do canvases reset?
Yes
How long until the next reset?
Canvases are usually reset when they're full, staff do not have a set date until right at the end of the canvas. Updates are posted in our Discord server when a reset date is settled upon.
Is there somewhere I can go to see past canvases?
We post past canvases in our Discord server. There is also a 3rd party website that chronicles past canvases and shows a lot of useful stats which can be found here.
How do I change my username color?
There is a "Username Color" option in the Settings panel near the bottom.
How do I get more info or contact staff?
Check the Info section (the icon top left) for more detailed info and links, or join our discord.
